Our Story

From humble beginnings to catering excellence

I started my career as a professional consultative seller, with strong Business Development and Account Management skills with a demonstrated history of working in the tech industries. With a Master of Business Administration (MBA) and PGPM (International Business) focused in Sales, Distribution, International Business, and Marketing Operations, General from Bangalore i achieved great success working with Startups like OLA and OYO. during my collaboration with these companies i saw a gap in the marketing and event organisation which led to the formation of La Luire Limited in 2022. In the past 3 years we have catered to some of the most diverse and complex organisations.
